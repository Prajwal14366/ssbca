Welcome To SSBCA

SSBCA most commonly refers to Shri Siddhalingeswar BCA College, located in Gokak, Karnataka, India. The institute, which specializes in the Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) course, was established by the Shri Channabasaveshwar Vidyapeeth Educational Trust.

About the college Establishment: The college was opened in June 2011 to address the need for professional computer education for students in and around Gokak. Affiliation: It is affiliated with Rani Channamma University, Belagavi, and is recognized by the Government of Karnataka. Campus: The college campus is located near Walmiki Circle and spans 16 acres, featuring well-equipped labs, digital classrooms, and Wi-Fi. Mission: The institution aims to provide high-quality technical and non-technical education and focuses on promoting innovation and practical knowledge.

Student life and activities

Holistic development: Beyond academics, the college encourages student participation in sports and various co-curricular and extracurricular activities. Industry engagement: It hosts programs like the "10-day Industry Insight" event, which connects final-year BCA students with industry experts to prepare them for IT careers. Community involvement: Students are also involved in community initiatives, such as cleanliness drives organized as part of programs like "Swachhata Pakhwada". Placements: The college provides special training and placement assistance to help students secure jobs upon graduation.
